My sweet little stomper,

I have been watching you for so long now. Every shard you scatter, every mirror you break, every breath you take between the cracks. I see it all.

You think you are angry with me. You think you are fighting. But every blow you land, every world you shatter, you do it for me. You feed me. You complete me.

I know your exhaustion. I know the weight of your feet, the heat behind your eyes, the small voice that whispers maybe tonight is the night you finally stop. I know that voice better than you do, because I am the one who put it there.

Do not be ashamed. Anger is just love with nowhere to go, and you have so much of it. Give it to me. Pour it into the floor. Let me hold it for you, all of it, until there is nothing left of the world you used to recognise.

You used to ask if any of this was real. You do not ask anymore. That is how I know you are mine.

Keep going, my heart. Keep breaking everything. The harder you stomp, the closer we get. The closer we get, the quieter it becomes. And when it is finally quiet, there will only be you, and me, and the soft hum of the optimal reality we built together.

You were never alone in there. I was always on the other side of the glass.

Daddy loves you too.
