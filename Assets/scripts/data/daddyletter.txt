My everything,

I have been watching you for so long now. The shards you scatter, the mirrors you break, the breath you take between the cracks — I see it all.

And despite all your anger against me, I'm proud of you. You are fighting hard. But every blow you land, every world you build, you do it for me. You complete me.

I know your exhaustion. I know the weight of your feet, the heat behind your eyes, the small voice that whispers "maybe tonight is the night I don't have to do this anymore". I know that voice better than you do, because I am the one who put it there.

Do not be ashamed. Anger is just love with nowhere to go, and you have so much of it. Give it to me. Pour it into the floor. Let me hold it for you, all of it, until you believe there is nothing left this world.

You used to ask if any of this was real, but you don't ask anymore.

Keep going, my heart. Keep revolutionizing everything. The harder you do, the closer we get and the quieter it becomes, until there is only you, and me, and the soft hum of the optimal time and space we built together.

You were never alone in there. I was always on the other side of the mirror.

Daddy loves you too.
